trale – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

trale is a body-weight diary app developed by QuantumPhysique. This privacy policy describes how trale handles your data.

Data collection and storage

trale does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter in the app — including body-weight measurements, your goal, and app settings — is stored exclusively on your device using the Android operating system's local storage mechanisms. Nothing is ever sent to our servers or any third-party service.

Internet access

trale does not connect to the internet for any purpose. The internet permission declared in the app manifest is required solely by the Flutter development tooling (e.g. hot-reload) and is never used in production builds.

Notifications

If you enable the optional reminder notifications, trale schedules local notifications entirely on your device using Android's alarm APIs (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED). No notification content is transmitted off-device.

Import and export

trale lets you import and export your measurement data as a file. This operation is performed locally on your device. You choose where to save or load the file using Android's file picker — trale does not access any other files on your device.

Third-party services

trale does not use any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or other third-party SDKs that could collect user data.

Children's privacy

trale does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date at the top.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
